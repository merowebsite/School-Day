School Day 2073


The school day in the golden jubilee year of a school is a momentous occasion that is eagerly awaited by students, staff, and the wider school community. It marks 50 years of the school's existence, an impressive milestone that is a cause for celebration. The day is usually filled with a range of activities and events that are designed to commemorate the school's history and achievements, as well as to look towards the future. This program is accompanied by the Rt. Honourable President Bidya Devi Bhandari. There may be speeches by alumni or other distinguished guests, performances by students, and presentations of awards and prizes. The day is also an opportunity for the school to showcase its facilities and programs to the wider community, and to reinforce the school's values and mission. Overall, the golden jubilee school day is a time to reflect on the past, celebrate the present, and look forward to a bright and promising future for the school.
